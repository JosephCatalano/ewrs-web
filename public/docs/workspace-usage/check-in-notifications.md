# Check-In Notifications

EWRS sends reminder emails when you have not checked into your booked workspace. If you do not check in by the required deadline, your booking will be automatically released and made available to other users.

![Check-in reminder email](/docs/assets/ewrs-manual/checkin-reminder-email.png)

## Notification schedule and check-in deadlines

Check-in requirements are standardized across all locations and divisions:

| Booking period    | Check-in deadline |
| ----------------- | ----------------- |
| Full-day booking  | 10:00 a.m.        |
| Morning booking   | 10:00 a.m.        |
| Afternoon booking | 1:00 p.m.         |

Reminder emails are sent to alert you before the check-in deadline:

| Booking period    | Reminder sent at |
| ----------------- | ---------------- |
| Full-day booking  | 9:30 a.m.        |
| Morning booking   | 9:30 a.m.        |
| Afternoon booking | 12:30 p.m.       |

> **Reminder:** You must check in as soon as you arrive at your workspace. If you do not check in by the deadline, your booking will be automatically cancelled and released to other users.

## Auto-cancellation

If you have not checked into your workspace by the check-in deadline, EWRS will automatically release your booking. This allows other users to book the workspace and ensures workspaces are used efficiently. When your booking is auto-cancelled, you will receive a notification email.

![Auto cancellation warning](/docs/assets/ewrs-manual/auto-cancel-warning.png)

## Email message

The reminder email notification indicates that you have not checked into your booked workspace and reminds you of the check-in deadline. If you fail to check in by the deadline, you will receive an auto-cancellation confirmation email.

![Auto cancellation email](/docs/assets/ewrs-manual/auto-cancel-email.png)
