# Check In

## Geo-Fenced Check-In
Geo-fencing is how EWRS verifies that you are physically at your booked location when you check in. You must be in the office to complete your check-in, the system will confirm you are on-site before allowing you to proceed. This ensures workspaces are only occupied by staff who are present.

## How to check in:

1. Open EWRS on your laptop when you arrive at your building.
3. Navigate to your booking and select **Check-In**.
4. Ensure you are connected to the OPS network, EWRS will automatically verify your location using your IP address.

![My Bookings tab showing the Check-In button](/docs/assets/ewrs-manual/my-bookings-cancel.png)

5. You are now checked into your workspace.

## Check-in deadlines (all locations):

**Full-day and morning bookings:** Must be checked in by **10:00 AM**
**Afternoon bookings:** Must be checked in by **1:00 PM**
If you miss your check-in deadline, your booking may be released and the workspace made available to others
